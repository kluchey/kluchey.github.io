What Needs To Happen To See Change

Voting With Your Wallet

Before microtransactions existed most games allowed players to unlock in-game items by playing the game and the process was not tedious. Video games are meant to be fun experiences and not labour intensive, money wasters. If you are not getting the same value back from the game as you are investing in it with real money, then quit the game. Recently, a new Star Wars game, Battlefront 2, released with the ability to purchase ‘loot crates’ that give players the chance at receiving huge advantages over others in-game. Do you agree with the idea that if someone spends more money on a game than you then they deserve to win more games? If you think this is unfair, do not buy games that let you do this; also known as a boycott or ‘voting with your wallet’.
